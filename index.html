Curatorial Narrative
策展叙事
The next economy will not be built on efficiency alone. It will be shaped by how deeply technology can sense, interpret, and respond to human emotion. Feeling Futures imagines the emotional architectures of tomorrow — where AI becomes a medium of empathy, care, and cultural resonance.
下一个经济时代不会仅仅建立在效率之上。它将取决于技术能多深刻地感知、理解和回应人类情感。「感知未来」想象明天的情感建筑——AI 不再是优化工具，而是共情、关怀与文化共鸣的媒介。

Why This, Why Now
为什么是现在

The emotional economy is the next frontier

情感经济是下一个前沿

We are entering an era where human feeling becomes both the medium and the market.

我们正进入一个人类情感既是媒介也是市场的时代。

01

The Emotional Economy

情感经济

Global markets are shifting from products to experiences, from features to feelings. Emotional design is becoming a core value driver.

全球市场正从产品转向体验，从功能转向感受。情感设计正成为核心价值驱动力。

02

AI as Emotional Medium

AI 作为情感媒介

Generative AI and affective computing are transforming how we design for feeling. AI is becoming the material of experience itself.

生成式 AI 和情感计算正改变我们为感受而设计的方式。AI 正成为体验本身的材料。

03

Future Lifestyles

未来生活方式

How will we rest, connect, heal, and celebrate in 2035? New emotional rituals mediated by AI, grounded in culture.

2035 年我们将如何休息、连接、疗愈和庆祝？以 AI 为媒介、扎根文化的新情感仪式。

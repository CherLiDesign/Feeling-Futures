Curatorial Narrative
策展叙事
The next economy will not be built on efficiency alone. It will be shaped by how deeply technology can sense, interpret, and respond to human emotion. Feeling Futures asks a generation of designers to imagine the emotional architectures of tomorrow — where AI becomes not a tool of optimization, but a medium of empathy, care, and cultural resonance.
下一个经济时代不会仅仅建立在效率之上。它将取决于技术能多深刻地感知、理解和回应人类情感。「感知未来」邀请新一代设计师去想象明天的情感建筑——在那里，AI 不再是优化的工具，而是共情、关怀与文化共鸣的媒介。

This workshop is a space between a research lab and a design biennale — where poetic vision meets innovation rigor, and where students learn to translate emotional intelligence into experiences the world can feel.

这个工作坊是研究实验室与设计双年展之间的一个空间——诗意愿景与创新严谨在此相遇，学生们将学会把情感智慧转化为世界可以感知的体验。

视觉方向

The visual identity should evoke warmth within technology — organic gradients meeting precise geometries, breath-like animations, and a palette that feels more like dawn light than screen glow.

视觉识别应在技术中唤起温度——有机渐变与精确几何相遇，呼吸般的动效，以及更像晨光而非屏幕光芒的色调。

Reference worlds: Olafur Eliasson's immersive environments, Neri Oxman's material futures, teamLab's emotional interfaces, the quiet futurism of Muji and Snow Peak.

参考世界：Olafur Eliasson 的沉浸式环境、Neri Oxman 的材料未来、teamLab 的情感界面、无印良品与 Snow Peak 的静谧未来主义。

Why This, Why Now
为什么是现在

The emotional economy is the next frontier of design innovation

情感经济是设计创新的下一个前沿

We are entering an era where human feeling becomes both the medium and the market. AI can now sense mood, interpret context, and generate responsive environments. The designers who understand this intersection will define the next generation of experiences.

我们正进入一个人类情感既是媒介也是市场的时代。AI 已能感知情绪、理解语境、生成响应式环境。理解这一交叉点的设计师，将定义下一代体验。

🌊

The Emotional Economy

情感经济

Global markets are shifting from products to experiences, from features to feelings. Emotional design is becoming a core value driver — in wellness, retail, hospitality, mobility, and urban life. China's "experience economy" (体验经济) is a national strategic direction.

全球市场正在从产品转向体验，从功能转向感受。情感设计正在成为核心价值驱动力——在健康、零售、酒店、出行和城市生活领域。中国的「体验经济」已成为国家战略方向。

🧠

AI as Emotional Medium

AI 作为情感媒介

Generative AI, affective computing, and multimodal sensing are transforming how we design for human feeling. AI is no longer just a backend engine — it is becoming the material of experience itself. Designers must learn to shape this material with intention and sensitivity.

生成式 AI、情感计算和多模态感知正在改变我们为人类感受而设计的方式。AI 不再只是后端引擎——它正在成为体验本身的材料。设计师必须学会带着意图和敏感性来塑造这种材料。

🏙

Future Lifestyles

未来生活方式

How will we rest, connect, heal, consume, and celebrate in 2035? The answers require designers who can imagine new emotional rituals — mediated by AI, grounded in culture, and scaled through experience platforms. This is the design space Feeling Futures opens.

2035 年我们将如何休息、连接、疗愈、消费和庆祝？答案需要能够想象新情感仪式的设计师——以 AI 为媒介、扎根文化、通过体验平台规模化。这就是「感知未来」所开启的设计空间。

Workshop Architecture
工作坊架构

Three movements, five sessions — from sensing to making

三个阶段，五次课程——从感知到创造

The workshop follows an emotional design methodology: begin with deep sensing, move through imaginative design, and arrive at tangible innovation. Each stage builds new muscles while respecting the creative strengths students already have.

工作坊遵循情感设计方法论：从深度感知开始，经历想象性设计，最终抵达可触摸的创新。每个阶段都在尊重学生已有创造力的基础上构建新能力。

Stage 01
阶段 01

Sensing the Now

感知当下

感知当下
Sensing the Now

Investigate the emotional texture of contemporary life. Map the feelings that technologies create, suppress, or transform. Build an emotional atlas of the present as the foundation for future design.

调查当代生活的情感肌理。映射技术所创造、压抑或转化的感受。构建当下的情感图谱，作为未来设计的基础。

  • S1 Emotional Landscape Mapping — research methodologies for sensing cultural moods, unmet emotional needs, and emerging desire patterns
  • S2 AI Sensing Workshop — hands-on exploration of affective computing, sentiment analysis, and multimodal emotion recognition as design research tools
  • S1 情感图景绘制——感知文化情绪、未被满足的情感需求和新兴欲望模式的研究方法
  • S2 AI 感知工作坊——动手探索情感计算、情绪分析和多模态情感识别作为设计研究工具
Stage 02
阶段 02

Designing Future States

设计未来状态

设计未来状态
Designing Future States

Transform emotional insights into speculative experience concepts. Learn to bridge visionary thinking with innovation structure — world-building meets value proposition.

将情感洞察转化为推测性体验概念。学习将远见思维与创新结构连接——世界构建遇见价值主张。

  • S3 Experience Concept Design — scenario building, emotional journey mapping, and experience architecture for AI-mediated futures
  • S4 Innovation Structuring — framing concepts as innovation projects: user validation, impact modeling, and competition-ready narrative building
  • S3 体验概念设计——场景构建、情感旅程映射和 AI 介导未来的体验架构
  • S4 创新结构化——将概念框架化为创新项目：用户验证、影响力建模和竞赛级叙事构建
Stage 03
阶段 03

Making It Real

让它成真

让它成真
Making It Real

通过快速原型制作和路演打磨将概念变为现实。产出同时可作为展览作品、竞赛参赛作品和概念验证演示的成果。

Student Outcomes
学生成果

Skills upgrade: from concept storytellers to experience innovators

能力升级：从概念叙事者到体验创新者

Students already know how to feel and imagine. This workshop teaches them to structure, validate, and deliver — transforming poetic sensibility into competition-winning innovation.

学生们已经懂得如何感受和想象。这个工作坊教他们如何结构化、验证和交付——将诗性感知力转化为赢得竞赛的创新。

01

Emotional Research Methods

情感研究方法

Move beyond intuition. Learn structured approaches to mapping emotional landscapes, identifying affective needs, and grounding creative concepts in real human insight.

超越直觉。学习结构化方法来映射情感图景、识别情感需求，并将创意概念扎根于真实的人类洞察。

02

AI Prototyping Fluency

AI 原型制作能力

Use generative AI, affective computing APIs, and creative coding as prototyping materials — not just as content generators, but as experience design mediums.

将生成式 AI、情感计算 API 和创意编程作为原型材料——不仅仅是内容生成器，而是体验设计的媒介。

03

Innovation Project Structuring

创新项目结构化

Frame creative concepts as innovation projects with clear value propositions, user validation logic, market positioning, and scalable impact narratives.

将创意概念框架化为创新项目，具备清晰的价值主张、用户验证逻辑、市场定位和可规模化的影响力叙事。

04

Competition-Ready Communication

竞赛级表达能力

Master the art of the innovation pitch: distilling visionary ideas into clear, compelling narratives for judges, investors, and cultural institutions.

掌握创新路演的艺术：将远见卓识的想法提炼为面向评委、投资者和文化机构的清晰而有说服力的叙事。

05

Cross-Media Experience Design

跨媒体体验设计

Design experiences that flow across app, space, VR, installation, and service — understanding how emotional continuity works across media boundaries.

设计跨越应用、空间、VR、装置和服务的体验——理解情感连续性如何跨越媒介边界。

06

Cultural Innovation Thinking

文化创新思维

Connect emotional design to larger cultural narratives — social impact, governance themes, and the "new economy" discourse that frames national innovation competitions.

将情感设计与更大的文化叙事相连——社会影响、治理主题，以及框定国家级创新竞赛的「新经济」话语体系。

Final Deliverables
最终交付物

Three output formats — each serving a different stage

三种产出形式——各服务于不同场景

Every team produces work that functions simultaneously as an exhibition piece, a competition entry, and a proof of concept. The deliverables are designed to travel across contexts.

每个团队产出的作品同时可作为展览作品、竞赛参赛作品和概念验证。交付物被设计为可跨场景使用。

Prototype

原型

Functional Experience Demo

功能体验演示

A working prototype that demonstrates the core emotional interaction — built with AI APIs, creative coding (p5.js, TouchDesigner), Figma, or VR tools.

一个展示核心情感交互的可工作原型——使用 AI API、创意编程（p5.js、TouchDesigner）、Figma 或 VR 工具构建。

Experience Video

体验视频

A 2–3 minute video that shows the experience in context — how it feels to encounter, use, and be changed by the designed interaction.

一段 2-3 分钟的视频，展示体验在真实场景中的样子——遭遇、使用这个交互设计并被其改变的感受。

Technical Architecture

一张清晰的图表，展示 AI、感知和体验层如何连接——在不需要完整工程的情况下证明可行性。

Pitch

Innovation Pitch Deck

创新路演文稿

8–12 slides following competition standards: problem, insight, concept, user validation, prototype, impact model, and team positioning.

8-12 页符合竞赛标准的演示文稿：问题、洞察、概念、用户验证、原型、影响力模型和团队定位。

One-Page Innovation Brief

Institutional Value
院系价值

How this workshop advances the department's strategic evolution

本工作坊如何推动院系的战略升级

Feeling Futures is designed not as an isolated event but as a catalyst — a model for how Art & Technology education can evolve toward innovation relevance without losing its creative soul.

「感知未来」不是一次孤立的活动，而是一个催化剂——一个艺术与科技教育如何在不失创造灵魂的前提下向创新方向演进的模型。

From Exhibition to Competition

从展览到竞赛

Students learn to frame their creative work as innovation projects — gaining the structural fluency needed for national competitions like Internet+, Challenge Cup, and international design innovation awards.

学生学会将创意作品框架化为创新项目——获得参加互联网+、挑战杯等国家级竞赛和国际设计创新奖项所需的结构化表达能力。

AI Integration with Integrity

有灵魂的 AI 融合

Rather than treating AI as a technical add-on, this workshop positions AI as a design material — teaching students to use it with the same intentionality they bring to any creative medium.

不是将 AI 视为技术附加物，而是将其定位为设计材料——教学生以对待任何创作媒介相同的意图性来使用 AI。

New Economy Alignment

新经济对齐
